The photographer

A fellow parent,
behind the lens

Hi, I'm Deep — a dad of two based in Cranbourne East, and the person behind Still & Golden. I have a three-year-old who keeps me on my toes and a brand new baby who has completely stolen my heart (and my sleep).

Photography has been my passion for over ten years. What started as a hobby grew into something I couldn't put down — capturing moments for friends and family, documenting milestones, finding the light in everyday life. This year, I decided to make it official.

I know firsthand how fast these early days go. My approach is gentle and unhurried — I work at your baby's pace, in a space that feels comfortable for your whole family. What you'll take home are warm, honest images that feel like you.
