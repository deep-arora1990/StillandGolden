Outfit & Colour Guide

For the most timeless images, we recommend choosing outfits from a soft, earthy palette — muted tones that complement natural light and keep the focus on your family rather than the clothing.

Recommended tones
Coordinate tones, don't match exactly — variety within the palette looks natural
Textures like linen, knit, and cotton photograph beautifully in natural light
Layers work well — cardigans, wraps, and soft jackets add warmth and dimension
Avoid: bright white, black, neon colours, or large bold patterns
