01 — Brand Foundation

Brand Essence

Still & Golden exists to capture the moments families will want to return to forever. The name says it all — still (quiet, unhurried, present) and golden (precious, warm, fleeting). Every word we write should reflect that.

Brand Personality

Warm
Write like you're talking to a friend, not a customer
Calm
Never salesy, never urgent, never pushy
Genuine
Personal stories over polished marketing speak
Quietly confident
Let the work speak — don't oversell
Emotionally aware
Acknowledge how big these moments are for families

Key Messages

1
Newborn sessions are time-sensitive — best between 5–14 days old. Book early.
2
Sessions are relaxed and led by your family — no stiff posing, no pressure.
3
These moments go fast — photographs are how you hold onto them.
4
Still & Golden is personal — Deep is a dad himself and understands what these photos mean.

02 — Voice Across Channels

Voice Across Channels

Instagram Captions

  • Lead with emotion or observation, not information
  • Short sentences. White space. Let the image breathe.
  • End with a soft call to action — never a hard sell
  • One or two emojis max, used intentionally (🤍 ✨ are on-brand)
  • Hashtags at the bottom, not in the caption body
Caption Formula
Emotional hook → brief observation → what this means → soft CTA

Website Copy

  • Conversational but refined — like a warm, articulate human wrote it
  • Second person ("you", "your family") to speak directly to the reader
  • Avoid photography jargon
  • Focus on the feeling, not the technical process

Email / Enquiry Responses

  • Friendly and personal — use the client's name
  • Respond warmly to their specific situation (due date, family details)
  • Keep it concise — busy new parents don't have time to read essays

Stories / Behind the Scenes

  • More casual and unfiltered than feed posts
  • Can use humour, especially around the realities of newborn/family life
  • Great place to show process, setup, or personal moments

03 — Language & Templates

Language & Templates

Language to Use

  • tiny · fleeting · unhurried · soft
  • quiet · golden · still
  • moments · details · story
  • remember · hold onto
  • little one · just arrived · brand new
  • real · honest · authentic · unposed
  • beautiful chaos (family sessions)

Language to Avoid

  • packages · slots · shoot
  • "book now!" — exclamation urgency
  • professional · high-quality
  • stunning results — clichés
  • "I offer..." — transactional
  • amazing · incredible · breathtaking

Caption Templates

Newborn — Detail / Abstract
[Tiny detail observation]. [What it represents emotionally]. [Soft CTA + contact]
Newborn — Parent with Baby
[What you see in the image — the emotion]. [Relatable truth for new parents]. [Soft CTA]
Family Session
[Honest, slightly playful observation about kids/family]. [What the photos capture]. [CTA]
Personal / Story Post
[Personal moment or story]. [What it means to you as a photographer and a dad]. [Connection to the work]

What We Never Do

Run discount promotions or "limited time offers"
Post low-quality or heavily filtered images
Write captions that are longer than they need to be
Use stock imagery or generic graphics
Compare ourselves to other photographers
The Still & Golden Test
Is this warm? Is this honest? Does it sound like a real person who genuinely cares about families?
If yes — post it. If not — rewrite it.
