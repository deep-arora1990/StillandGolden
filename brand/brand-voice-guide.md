# Still & Golden ‚Äî Tone of Voice & Style Guide

---

## Brand Essence

Still & Golden exists to capture the moments families will want to return to forever. The name says it all ‚Äî *still* (quiet, unhurried, present) and *golden* (precious, warm, fleeting). Every word we write should reflect that.

---

## Brand Personality

| Trait | What it means in practice |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **Warm** | Write like you're talking to a friend, not a customer |
| **Calm** | Never salesy, never urgent, never pushy |
| **Genuine** | Personal stories over polished marketing speak |
| **Quietly confident** | Let the work speak ‚Äî don't oversell |
| **Emotionally aware** | Acknowledge how big these moments are for families |

## Voice Across Channels

### Instagram Captions
- Lead with emotion or observation, not information
- Short sentences. White space. Let the image breathe.
- End with a soft call to action ‚Äî never a hard sell
- One or two emojis max, used intentionally (ü§ç ‚ú® are on-brand)
- Hashtags at the bottom, not in the caption body

**Formula:**
> *Emotional hook ‚Üí brief observation ‚Üí what this means ‚Üí soft CTA*

### Website Copy
- Conversational but refined ‚Äî like a warm, articulate human wrote it
- Second person ("you", "your family") to speak directly to the reader
- Avoid photography jargon
- Focus on the feeling, not the technical process

### Email / Enquiry Responses
- Friendly and personal ‚Äî use the client's name
- Respond warmly to their specific situation (newborn due date, family details)
- Keep it concise ‚Äî busy new parents don't have time to read essays

### Stories / Behind the Scenes
- More casual and unfiltered than feed posts
- Can use humour, especially around the realities of newborn/family life
- Great place to show process, setup, or personal moments

## Language To Use

- *tiny*, *fleeting*, *unhurried*, *soft*, *quiet*, *golden*, *still*
- *moments*, *details*, *story*, *remember*, *hold onto*
- *family*, *little one*, *brand new*, *just arrived*
- *real*, *honest*, *authentic*, *unposed*
- *beautiful chaos* (for family sessions)

## Language To Avoid

- *packages*, *slots*, *shoot* (say *session* instead)
- *book now!* or any exclamation-point urgency
- *professional*, *high-quality*, *stunning results* ‚Äî clich√©s
- *I offer...* ‚Äî too transactional
- Overuse of superlatives (*amazing*, *incredible*, *breathtaking*)

## What We Never Do

- Run discount promotions or "limited time offers"
- Post low-quality or heavily filtered images
- Write captions that are longer than they need to be
- Use stock imagery or generic graphics
- Compare ourselves to other photographers

---

## The Still & Golden Test

Before publishing anything, ask:
> *Is this warm? Is this honest? Does it sound like a real person who genuinely cares about families?*

If yes ‚Äî post it. If not ‚Äî rewrite it.
